# Operators

 * `&&` - Boolean and
 * `||` - Boolean or
 * `&` - Bitwise and
 * `|` - Bitwise or
 * `==` - Equal
 * `!=` - Not equal
 * `<` - Less than
 * `<=` - Less then equal
 * `>` - Greater than
 * `>=` - Greater than equal
 * `+` - Addition OR string concatenation
 * `-` - Subtraction OR negation
 * `/` - Division
 * `*` - Multiplication
 * `**` - Power
 * `%` - Modulo
 * `? (success) : (fail)` - Ternary
 * `()` - Grouping
